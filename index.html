About

The home of modern procurement

BuyrWorld exists because procurement tooling has been stuck for a decade: clunky suites built for auditors, or generic AI that's never seen an RFQ. We're building the platform buyers deserve — modern, fast and genuinely useful on a Tuesday afternoon when three quotes land at once.

Founder Josh Frost is a CIPS-qualified Senior Procurement Manager with 12+ years across supply chain, aerospace and manufacturing: supplier development on flight-critical parts, multi-year negotiations, category strategies and risk reviews that had to survive real scrutiny. BuyrWorld packages that experience — then multiplies it with AI.
