Price-Increase Defender

Paste the supplier's price-increase letter or email. The Defender scores how well-justified it really is, exposes the weak points, and drafts your rebuttal and counter-proposal.

Negotiation Simulator

Set the scene, then negotiate against an AI playing the supplier's sales director — it will anchor, flinch and nibble like the real thing. Say your piece, trade hard, and when you're done hit End & score me for your scorecard out of 100.

Meeting Notes Summariser

Paste your raw meeting notes — scrappy bullet points, half-sentences, whatever you scribbled. You'll get clean, email-ready minutes: summary, decisions, and an action list with owners, departments, tasks and due dates (anything not stated is marked TBC rather than invented). One tap to copy for email.

Quote Comparator

Drop in 2–4 supplier quotes in any mixture of Excel, PDF, Word or CSV. You'll get a comprehensive review — quote summary, normalised landed-cost comparison with the working shown, weighted scoring, risks and the questions to ask each supplier — downloadable as a BuyrWorld-branded report.

Works with text-based PDFs (not scans/photos). Anonymise anything sensitive — file text is sent to Buyr AI for the comparison.

RFQ Generator

Answer the brief, get a professional Request for Quotation issued in your company's name — downloadable as a BuyrWorld-watermarked PDF, ready to send to suppliers.
